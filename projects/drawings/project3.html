Step 1 – Concept Design

Anatomical Sketch Project

The goal of this project was to create a precise anatomical hand sketch highlighting muscle structure and proportion. The sketch emphasizes line weight, shading, and correct anatomical alignment for educational use.

Step 2: Initial Sketch

The first stage began with light pencil sketches, focusing on proportion and muscle curvature. Cross-contour lines were used to visualize depth and surface geometry before adding texture.

Step 3: Final Rendering

Shading & Detailing

The final version includes realistic shading using graphite and digital touch-up for contrast correction. Highlights were emphasized to display form, and the sketch was digitized for integration in the biomedical study guide.

Step 4: ReadMe / Conclusion

This drawing demonstrates artistic precision and understanding of biomedical anatomy. The project reinforces the intersection between art and medical illustration. Future works may include colorized digital overlays and 3D anatomical visualization.
