Geometry Meshing Simulation ReadMe

Step 1 – Geometry Setup

Biomedical Stent Model

3D stent geometry was imported from SolidWorks and prepared for analysis using ANSYS SpaceClaim. Symmetry and fillets were optimized for mesh quality.

Step 2: Meshing

Fine tetrahedral mesh applied at critical regions to capture stress gradients. Element size refined through convergence study for accurate results.

Step 3: Simulation Setup & Results

Boundary Conditions & Analysis

Fixed end constraints and internal pressure loads applied. von-Mises stress distribution was evaluated to assess material safety factors.

Step 4: ReadMe / Conclusion

This simulation highlights mechanical strength and deformation patterns of a stent under pressure. Future studies will couple fluid-structure interaction for realistic flow conditions.
